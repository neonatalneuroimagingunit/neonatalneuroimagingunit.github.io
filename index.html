Neonatal Neuroimaging Unit
The Neonatal Neuroimaging Unit (NNU) is an academic research lab based in Rovereto, Italy.

We are dedicated to cognitive neuroscience research on human newborns and we are part of the Center for Mind/Brain Sciences (CIMeC) at Università degli Studi di Trento. The NNU is hosted by Unità Operativa di Ostetricia e Ginecologia, the maternity ward within Ospedale Santa Maria del Carmine.
